Autobiography - A Self Memoir

At a young age, my greedy biological family sold me to a Dutch traveling circus where I was raised on ape milk and coerced to do backflips for euros. The bars couldn't hold me long. After escaping with the French gibbon I was seeing at the time, I traveled the world. Her name was Darla. But too soon, I was swindled into giving up Darla to some balding men in a high-stakes bocce ball game. I never saw her again. Once the portraits Darla and I had painted together were shredded and I had recuperated emotionally, I set off for new lands: the USA.
